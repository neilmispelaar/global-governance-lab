Global Governance Lab at ISID

The Global Governance Research-to-Practice Lab at ISID is bridging the gap between academics, practitioners and policy-makers on issues related to global governance and international development. It is built around three interconnected nodes: natural resource conflicts and trade-offs, migration and refugees, and governance.
