Marie-Eve Yergeau is a Research Associate at the Institute for the Study of International Development (ISID) at McGill University. She has a PhD in Development Economics from the Université de Sherbrooke and the Université de Montpellier (joint program). She is specialized in poverty and inequality analysis. Her research interests also include: environmental economics, food security, applied microeconometrics and survey methods. She has conducted field work in Mali and in Nepal. As a Research Associate at ISID, she also works on the questions of migration and refugees, natural resource conflicts and trade-offs, and governance.
